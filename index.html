Poverty is complex, and the reasons surrounding its existence are deeply historical and multifaceted. At Poverty Action, we believe that poverty can be solved through progressive and inclusive policy legislation. However, in order to pass anti-poverty reforms that are both equitable and sustainable, we must first understand the scope of poverty in our state.

This interactive map allows users to explore the social, economic, and historical elements behind poverty in Washington state.

Our map shows the spread of poverty through a variety of factors, and includes stories of people affected by poverty in our state. In order to make the most of your time, we invite you to take the tour of the state of poverty in Washington.
